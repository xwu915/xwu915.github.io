About

Xiao Wu, MD is a resident in Interventional Radiology at the UCSF School of Medicine, committed to thoughtful, evidence-driven care. Her research centers on outcomes and cost-effectiveness in interventional radiology, as well as neurovascular imaging, with a focus on translating evidence into clinical practice.
